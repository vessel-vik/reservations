What changed from today

  • 3 tabs → 1 Finance tab — Accounting (3), VAT (4), Expenses (5) collapse into one
  • KPI strip always visible — Revenue · Expenses · Profit · VAT Due, with period selector
  • Budget alert banner — appears automatically when any category exceeds its monthly budget
  • Budget meters — colour-coded progress bars per category (green → amber → red)
  • Receipt column — thumbnail icon shows an invoice image is attached; click to preview
  • Edit button — finally wired up, pre-fills the expense drawer
  • Sections: Expenses · VAT · Reports — VAT uses the existing VATDashboard; Reports adds the trend chart + export
