Menu & Stock CMS — Which editing pattern?

Three approaches for the admin product management experience

A

Inline Editing + Drawer ⭐ Recommended

The product list is a live, editable table. Stock counts, prices, and availability toggle directly in the row. Clicking a product opens a full drawer for complex fields (ingredients, allergens, modifiers, image). Inspired by the Minimart Sanity SDK pattern.

B

Full-Page Editor

Product list is read-only. Clicking any item navigates to a dedicated editor page at /admin/menu/[id]. Full edit form with all fields, image upload, modifiers, and stock. Clean and simple but requires page navigation per item.

C

Split-Panel CMS

Left column: scrollable product list. Right column: editor panel that updates when you click any item. No page navigation. Similar to email clients or Contentful's layout.
